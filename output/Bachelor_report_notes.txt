The dataset covers 2018 to 2025 for 8 selected Ontario CMAs.
The analysis focuses on Bachelor apartment rents.
The overall mean rent is 949.83 dollars.
The highest average rent was observed in Toronto CMA at 1287.62 dollars.
The lowest average rent was observed in Greater Sudbury/Grand Sudbury CMA at 776.38 dollars.
Average Bachelor rent changed by 46.93 percent over the study period.
The t-test comparing university and non-university cities produced a p-value of 8.032e-06 .
The structural break test at 2022 produced a p-value of 1.805e-05 .
